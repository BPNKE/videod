How to Find the Video URL:

  1. Open the video page (videyd.com) in your browser.
  2. Right-click the video and select "Inspect" or press F12 to open Developer Tools.
  3. Go to the "Network" tab, filter by "Media," and play the video to capture the URL (ends in .mp4, .m3u8, etc.).
  4. Copy the full URL and paste it above.
  5. If no URL appears, the video may be protected. See alternative tools below.

Alternative Tools:

If the direct URL doesn’t work, try:

  • Getfvid or SaveFrom.net (online downloaders).
  • Browser extensions like Video DownloadHelper.
  • Command-line tools like yt-dlp (e.g., run yt-dlp https://www.videyd.com/e/L9TG40Lm?lv1=doodlix1.org).
